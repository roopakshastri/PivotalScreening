About Us

Reliable, secure, and efficient background verification for confident hiring.

Pivotal Screening provides comprehensive background verification solutions designed to support accurate, compliant, and efficient hiring decisions. Our processes are built around consistency and reliability to minimize discrepancies and deliver dependable results.

Through structured methodologies and optimized workflows, we maintain fast turnaround times without compromising the depth or integrity of verification. Our services cover employment, education, address, and criminal background checks to provide complete insights for informed decision-making.

Data security and confidentiality are central to our operations. All information is handled with strict safeguards and in alignment with applicable legal and regulatory requirements, ensuring compliance with modern data protection standards.

Our verification processes are designed for transparency, offering clear status tracking and detailed, audit-ready reports for every case. Pivotal Screening integrates seamlessly with existing systems, enabling smooth onboarding and scalable operations.

With a focus on customization, operational efficiency, and consistent quality delivery, we ensure a seamless experience for both organizations and candidates while maintaining responsive support throughout the verification lifecycle.

Our Services

End-to-end verification solutions tailored for every stage of the hiring process.

Court Record Check (Civil + Criminal)

Verify legal history across both civil and criminal court records.

Education Verification

Confirm academic qualifications directly from institutions to ensure authenticity.

Identity Verification (ID Check)

Authenticate identity documents to confirm candidate authenticity.

Employment Verification

Validate previous employment details including role, tenure, and performance.

Employment via PF Number

Verify employment history using provident fund records for accurate validation.

Credit Check

Assess financial reliability and creditworthiness to support informed hiring decisions.

Police Verification

Official police background verification to ensure candidate credibility and safety.

Database Checks (Global / Watchlists)

Search global databases and watchlists to identify potential risk indicators.

Address Verification

Verify residential address through physical or digital verification methods.

Directorship Check

Check involvement in company directorships and business affiliations.

Reference Check

Collect professional feedback from previous employers and colleagues.

Global Background Verification

Comprehensive international background screening across multiple regions.

Professional License Verification

Confirms that required licenses or certifications are valid, active, and issued by authorized bodies.
