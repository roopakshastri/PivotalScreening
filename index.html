About Us

Pivotal Screening provides extensive background checks that are intended to create accurate, compliant, and efficient hiring decisions through their comprehensive background verification solutions. The entire process of producing a verified candidate will be one of your total confidence levels because Pivotal has built every aspect around consistency and reliability so that they can minimize discrepancies and produce only the most accurate results possible. Utilizing patterned methodology and optimized workflows with fast delivery times, the integrity and depth of Pivotal's verification processes will never be compromised. Every service provided from educational verification, employment verification, Address Verification to Criminal Background checks will provide you with all the information you need to make well-informed hiring decisions. The safety and security of all data handled is paramount to the operation of Pivotal Screening. All data collected is subject to stringent safeguards and handled in an appropriate manner meeting all legal and regulatory obligations; including applicable data protection standards. In addition, Pivotal's verification processes have been designed to provide clients with complete transparency via the status of their verification along with complete audit-ready reporting for each case. The Pivotal Screening suite of services has been programmed to integrate seamlessly into your existing applicant tracking systems to allow organizations of any size to experience seamless onboarding and scalable oversight of their workforce. Pivotal Screening offers candidates non-intrusive and seamless verification processes while delivering timely updates/support for clients throughout their entire verification lifecycle. Pivotal Screening seeks to ensure a customized service that combines processes/clarity/compliance/cost-effectiveness/operational efficiencies/and consistent delivery of quality results so that organizations can be assured that their hiring decisions will remain based upon accurate-and-verified-information.
